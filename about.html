About Us

Our team is Cool Beans and we are composed of three first-year students at Stony Brook University: Amelia, Juan, and Jared. This is our first time attending a Hackathon. We come from Queens, NY and are hoping to learn more about programming and test our knowledge during this Hackathon.

About Our Project

Our intentions with this project were to create an interface that organizes patient information in such a way that could be used for hospitals and clinics. The Home page displays the various patients that the facility had encountered during its use. The Patient Information page is used to insert a new patient and their information into the facility's database.
